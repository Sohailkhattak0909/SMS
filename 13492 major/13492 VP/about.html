KK Model School Management System

Features

  1. Sign In and Sign Out: Users can securely sign in and sign out to access the system, ensuring data privacy and accountability.
  2. Dashboard: The dashboard provides an overview of essential statistics, including total student strength, gender distribution (male and female), and class-wise
    enrollment. This feature enables administrators to quickly grasp vital information about the school's demographics.
  3. Student Portal: The student portal is a central hub for student information management. Users can view detailed student profiles, register new students, and
    update or delete student records as necessary. This feature empowers administrators to maintain accurate and up-to-date student data effortlessly.
  4. Examination Module: The examination module facilitates efficient management of assessments. Users can view batch-wise toppers, add and update student
    marks, and display examination results seamlessly. This feature promotes transparency and enables educators to track student performance effectively.
  5. Time Table Management: The time table module simplifies scheduling by providing class-wise time tables that are easily editable. Users can add, update, and
    delete class schedules, ensuring timely and organized academic planning.

Additional Features

  • Night and Light Mode: Customize your viewing experience with the option to switch between light and dark modes, ensuring comfort and convenience for users
    in any lighting condition.
  • Print Functionality: Easily print important documents such as examination results and class timetables for record-keeping or distribution purposes.
